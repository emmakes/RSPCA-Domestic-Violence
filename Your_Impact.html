According to the RSPCA website;

$25 - could provide an orphaned animal with essentials like a bed,
litter tray and toys for enrichment.

$50 - could provide medicine for an animal recovering from surgery.

$100 - could subsidise the cost of prosecuting a cruelty offender in court.

$200 - could desex a dog or cat so that they can be adopted.

You would be helping abandoned, neglected, sick and surrendered animals in your local community.
